Why Jarabacoa?

Jarabacoa, where it is always Spring!

Jarabacoa, Dominican Republic is known as the "City of Everlasting Spring" because of its consistent warm temperatures and abundant rainfall throughout the year.Jarabacoa has a tropical rainforest climate with warm days and cool nights, and temperate days and cold nights in the winter. The average temperature is between 60°F and 72°F.

Jarabacoa is in the center of the Central Range of the Dominican Republic, at a high elevation in the interior. It's close to Pico Duarte, the Caribbean's highest peak at 10,164 ft. Jarabacoa is surrounded by three large rivers: the Baiguate, Jimenoa, and Yaque del Norte. Visitors can enjoy activities like rafting, tubing, kayaking, and canoeing on these rivers.Jarabacoa offers many outdoor activities, including hiking, horseback riding, biking, and exploring the area's natural environment. Visitors can also see fields of flowers and strawberries, and visit the Jarabacoa Mountain Village.
